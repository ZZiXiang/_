Original Performer:　伦桑 Lun Sang

Lyrics written by : Bye

Translation: 世说卿语 Shi Shuo Qing Yu


	烟雨行舟 
	Yan Yu Xing Zhou （Rowing in the Misty Rain)

　　烟雨入江南 
	Misty rain entering the South

　　山水如墨染
	Landscape as ink dyeing

　　宛若丹青未干
	like painting to drying

　　提笔然 点欲穿
	lifting pen then dripping

　　行舟临秀川
	boating to graceful lands

　　画鷁推清澜
	laurel boat cleaving waves

　　缱绻怡然
	everlating joy

　　天色沉靛蓝
	indigo sky 

　　波光似锦缎
	sparking like brocade

　　缀着零星白帆
	Dotting with white sails

　　诗情满 千卷难
	Poetic views, hard to convey

　　渔舟齐桅杆
	Fishing boats, parallel mast

　　鸳鸯凭舷栏
	Mandarin duck couple is swimming beside

　　琴瑟相伴
	Melody is around

　　一叶轻船
	A single boat

　　一双桨悠懒
	A pair oars rowing

　　一绵江风微拂素罗衫
	a breeze stirs plain dress

　　渔火点点聚散
	Fishing fire meet and apart

　　欸乃声声浅淡
	Oars sound fade away

　　天近晚
	Day to night

　　炊烟袅飘沿斑
	Smoke rouse along the bank

　　一叶轻船
	A single boating 

　　一双人倚揽
	A couple leans together

　　一曲烟雨行舟太缓慢
	Sailing slowly in the misty rain

　　执手相看
	Holding hand and staring

　　把酒当歌言欢
	Singing and confinding in wine

　　红尘路漫漫
	Love is long to run

　　愿今生与你共览
	Keep me acompany this life

　　暮霭渐褪暗
	Mist goes in deep

　　胭脂余味淡
	Rouge becomes faint

　　我支着竹绢伞
	I am holding umbrella

　　你闲摆花团扇
	You are playing a flower fan

　　浪儿晃曳慢
	Waves rise and fall

　　夜垂云流缓
	Clouds drift slowly at night fall
